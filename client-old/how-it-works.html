Workflow Overview

From Client Prompt to Dynamic Renders in Six Simple Steps

Learn how ArchFlow AI simplifies the architectural planning pipeline, saving civil engineers and architects hours of drafting work.

1
Step 01

Initialize Your Design Project

Create a project file and enter context parameters. Define client names, project categories (duplex house, luxury villa, residential apartments) and site locations.

2
Step 02

Configure Plot & Room Specifications

Define layout variables such as width, length, corner status, facing orientations, road layouts, floors count, bedrooms, bathrooms, pooja spaces, and parking setups. Add custom guidance prompts for special designs.

3
Step 03

AI Generates 2D Blueprints & 3D Render Swatches

The AI engine parses requirements and creates proportional 2D floor plans. Simultaneously, it generates exterior concepts reflecting different pricing plans (Budget, Modern, Luxury).

4
Step 04

Compare Style Variations Side-by-Side

Present visual mockups representing different budgets to the client. Compare layout elevations (Budget Friendly vs Luxury cantilevers) and select a layout option.

5
Step 05

Refine in the 2D Layout Canvas and 3D Viewer

Adjust details. Change room labels or drag walls on the 2D blueprint canvas. Tweak facade textures (concrete, timber, brick) and lighting angles inside the 3D viewer.

6
Step 06

Compile Presentation & Export Center

Combine generated plan drawings, selected 3D elevations, materials swatches, and Vastu analysis into a branded document with your company logo to print or share as a review link.
